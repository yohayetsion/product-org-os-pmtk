About

The first AI-powered implementation
of the PMTK Product Manager's Toolkit

Product Org OS — PMTK Edition brings Blackblot's proven product management methodology into Claude Code as a living, interactive operating system. Six AI agents work as your product management team, each mastering a distinct PMTK role.

With 63 skills mapping to PMTK blueprint templates, 12 knowledge packs encoding methodology fundamentals, and a context layer preserving organizational memory across sessions — this is product management methodology made actionable through AI.

$ /market-requirements "Enterprise CRM"
# Product Planner generates MRD

$ @product-marketer position against Salesforce
# Marketer creates positioning doc

$ @pmtk evaluate launch readiness
# Multi-agent assessment

Methodology

The PMTK Dual-Track Model

Product Planning and Product Marketing run in parallel. Two tracks, eight phases, one unified product lifecycle.

Product
Planning

Planning

Describe market problems and needs. Identify what customers require.

Definition

Define solutions to market problems. Features, roadmap, requirements.

Development

Build solutions that solve market problems. Pricing and delivery.

Maintenance

Sales channel support, product revisions, win/loss analysis.

Product
Marketing

Evaluation

Examine opportunities to serve the market. Business case analysis.

Strategy

Formulate market approach. Positioning, messaging, value props.

Readiness

Prepare market tactics and MarCom activities. Collateral, launch prep.

Execution

Deliver value and build competitive advantage. Campaigns, reviews.

Capabilities

Key Features

Everything you need to run a structured, methodology-driven product management practice with AI.

📄

Document Intelligence

Create, Update, or Find any PMTK deliverable. Skills support three modes, so documents evolve naturally across the product lifecycle.

👥

PMTK Role Model

Agents match Blackblot's PM Team and PD Team exactly. Product Planner, Marketer, Architect, Sales Engineer, MarCom Manager, and Director of Products.

🧠

Organizational Memory

The context layer preserves decisions, feedback, and learnings across sessions. Auto-context injection gives agents awareness of past work.

🏛️

Meeting Mode

The PMTK Gateway spawns multiple agents for cross-functional collaboration. Each agent speaks in first person before a synthesis.

🔌

MCP Integration

Connect to Jira, Slack, Confluence, and more when available. Graceful fallback to markdown when tools are not connected.

📚

12 Knowledge Packs

PMTK methodology encoded as reference material: Flow Model, Action Model, Product Frames, Problem Echelon, Glossary, and more.
